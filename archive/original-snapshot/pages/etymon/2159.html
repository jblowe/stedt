Reconstructed mesoroots below:
  • PKar *pleᴬ SLIPPERY

This root was set up as *ble in STC #141, on the basis of the Kanauri and Digaro forms. Firm cognates have since been discovered in Karenic and Tamangic. A number of forms point to a variant with final *‑l, including Pattani and Lushai (Mizo) (such a variant is also hypothesized as possible in Proto-Tamangic). The Cuona form might reflect a further variant with a velar suffix. See HPTB:204.
