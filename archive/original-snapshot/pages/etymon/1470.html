#1470 PTB *s-mar GOLD / YELLOW / BUTTER / OIL (provisional)

Reconstructed mesoroots below:
  • TGTM *ᴮmar GOLD
  • TGTM *ᴬmar BUTTER

The *s‑ prefix is reconstructed here on the basis of the Newar and the Nusu (Central) forms.

Many forms, especially in Qiangic, Tamangic, and Tani, seem to be borrowings from Tibetan.

Cf. also #1441 PTB *mwa‑ŋ FAT.

For the semantic connection between YELLOW and BUTTER, cf. Mandarin 黃油 huángyóu ‘butter’ (literally, “yellow oil”).
