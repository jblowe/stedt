#8 PTB *t(s)ak BODY
Reconstructed mesoroots below:
  • PKC *tak-shaa BODY

This etymon has reflexes ranging from an affricate ts‑ (Lotha), to a simple dental stop t‑ or th‑, to a dental sibilant (Mizo, Nocte), to an h‑ (Meithei, Moyon, Maring). [For a similar range of reflexes, cf. #2742 PTB *(t)sum MORTAR.

We can assume a development in Meithei from *ts‑ to *s‑ to h‑; the Moyon and Maring forms might well be borrowings from Meithei.

VanBik reconstructs a binome *tak‑shaa for Proto-Kuki-Chin, where the second element is the general etymon PTB *sya MEAT / FLESH / ANIMAL.
