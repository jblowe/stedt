#776 PTB *p(l)a-n ⪤ *p(w)a-n PALM / SOLE
Reconstructed mesoroots below:
  • PTani *lak-pro PALM (of hand)
  • PCN *m-ph(w)a FOOT (PART)

STC #418 reconstructs *pwa or *bwa. A similar but probably distinct root is #821 PTB *r‑pak LEAF / LEAFLIKE PART / FLAT OBJECT / PALM / SOLE, which underlie forms like Mizo kut‑phaʔ ‘palm’, ke‑phaʔ ‘sole’ and Karbi ri‑pak ~ ri‑pek ‘palm’, keŋ‑pak ‘sole’.

For now, we are treating the Proto-Tani form as a reflex of this root.
