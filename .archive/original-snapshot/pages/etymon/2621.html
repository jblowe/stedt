Some reflexes of this extremely widespread etymon reflect the minimal form *ruk; others descend from a *kr‑ cluster; while still others come from a *d‑r‑ combination.

The PLB form *C‑krokᴸ is reconstructed with a voiced prefix (represented by the cover symbol C‑), since its reflexes derive from the Proto-Loloish low-stopped tone, which can only come from a *voiced initial sequence. Cf. Matisoff 1972: #35.

For additional discussion of the numeral SIX, see Matisoff 1995 (“Numerals”), pp. 193-197.
