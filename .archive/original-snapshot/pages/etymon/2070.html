#2070 PTB *ruŋ HOLE / ORIFICE / EAR
Reconstructed mesoroots below:
  • PTani *ruŋ HOLE / DENT

This root is well-attested in Tani, and probably also in Qiangic and Central Naga.

This root occurs as second element in compounds with #811 PTB *r/g‑na EAR / HEAR / LISTEN / ASK. There is a fortuitous resemblance between this etymon and #814 PTB *m/g‑(r)wa‑ŋ/k/t HORN / ANGLE / CORNER, which has a certain specious plausibility since the ears are located on the sides of the head like horns.
