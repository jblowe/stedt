Search syntax & examples

Plain words match forms, meanings, and language names. Commas separate alternatives (frog, snail); inside a field, quote the alternatives together (gloss:"frog, snail"). Quote a value containing spaces. Narrow one axis with field:value — fields combine: try gloss:dog subgroup:Kiranti; repeating a field requires both at once (gloss:body gloss:hair). Every example below is runnable:

gloss:hand
the meaning
form:mruk
the form itself
language:Lotha
the language name
subgroup:"Central Loloish"
a Stammbaum subtree by name, abbreviation (PKC), or number (2.3)
group:Kuki-Chin
exactly that group, none of the subgroups beneath it (same keys, matched whole)
source:TBL
records from matching sources (abbreviation or author/title)
pos:v
part of speech (v, n, adj…)
pform:lak
a reconstruction's form
pgloss:hand
a reconstruction's meaning
tag:695
forms tagged to etymon #695
