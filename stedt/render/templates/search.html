Search syntax

Plain words match forms, meanings, and language names. Commas separate alternatives (frog, snail). Quote a value containing spaces. Narrow one axis with field:value. Fields combine, each example below is runnable:

gloss:hand
the meaning
form:mruk
the form itself
language:Lotha
the language name
subgroup:"Central Loloish"
a Stammbaum subtree by name, abbreviation (PKC), or number (2.3)
source:TBL
records from matching sources (abbreviation or author/title)
pos:v
part of speech (v, n, adj…)
proto:lak
a reconstruction's form (pgloss: its meaning)
tag:695
forms tagged to etymon #695
